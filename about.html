About CORE

CORE (Cipher Operated Rocket Execution) is a cybersecurity-based simulation system that showcases how encryption, multi-factor authentication, and secure communication are used in critical launch protocols.

What it does?

CORE simulates a high-security rocket launch that requires:


  • Password & OTP login
  • YubiKey hardware authentication
  • Real-time encrypted backend communication

Our Goal

At CORE, our goal is to revolutionize the way rocket operations are managed by integrating state-of-the-art cryptographic methods. We aim to enhance security, improve efficiency, and provide a seamless user experience for all stakeholders involved in rocket launches.

We believe that by leveraging advanced technology, we can significantly reduce the risks associated with rocket operations and ensure successful missions.
